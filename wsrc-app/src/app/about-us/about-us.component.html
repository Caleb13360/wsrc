What is the
World Sim Racing Competition?

The World Sim Racing Competition (WSRC) is the only iRacing competition where
racers compete for money weekly: No team needed, no minimum skill barrier
to enter. Simply race, and if you're fast enough you'll win cash prizes!

If you're fast enough, you deserve the chance to turn your passion into a living."
- Jimmy Grills, Race Commentator

Our Mission

At the World Sim Racing Championship (WSRC), our mission is to make competitive racing accessible to everyone. We believe that the thrill of racing and the opportunity to win should not be limited to professionals or those with elite teams.

WSRC provides a platform where anyone with a passion for racing can compete, test their skills, and win cash prizes. By eliminating the barriers of traditional competitions, we aim to create a fair, inclusive, and exciting environment where racers of all levels can chase their dreams and enjoy the adrenaline of competition.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the race I've registered for?

You will be sent an email with the server password from Matcherino 30 minutes before the scheduled event start. The race server will be avaible to join in iracing 15 minutes before scheduled event start as the same name as the race.

What happens when I win money in a race?

After winning, login to Matcherino and follow their instructions for withdrawing your winnings.

Are there any requirements for me to enter a race?

The only requirement is you must have an active iRacing subscription and already own the car and track combination being used for the event. Beyond that, registration is on a first-come, first-served basis—so make sure to secure your spot quickly before the race fills up!
