Introducing Racer Points

The Racer Points Challenge is here to celebrate consistency, dedication, and improvement! While the top 3 finishers in each race take home cash prizes, positions 4-20 earn Racer Points, with higher points awarded to better placements.

Each week, the top 10 drivers on the Racer Points event win a free race entry, giving everyone a chance to stay competitive and keep racing.
